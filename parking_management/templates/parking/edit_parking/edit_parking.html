How does it work?

First step - Enter your parking location on Google Maps.

Second step - Click right on the red marker.

Third step - Click left on the top row, it will copy the values

Fourth step - Paste the first value in "latitude" field, and your second value in "longitude"

Note - The values are longer than they appear on Google map. For accuracy, it is better to copy and paste them rather than enter them manually.

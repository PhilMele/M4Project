What is this?

In this field, you apply the duration (in hour) this rate will apply until.

Example: If you want a rate to apply for the first 1 hour, then add "1", if you want this rate to apply for the first 2 hours add "2"

This rate will then serve as a multiplier. If a user stayed 2 hours, they will be charged the rate attached to 2 hours.

The highest duration will be used for any duration exceeding this timeframe: if 2 hours is the maximum rate range, the hourly fee applicable to 2 hours will be applied to fee calculation for 3 or 4 hours.
